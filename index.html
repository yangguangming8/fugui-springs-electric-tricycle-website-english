About Fugui Springs

Your Trusted Partner in Electric Mobility Solutions

Founded in 2005, Fugui Springs has grown to become one of China's leading manufacturers of electric tricycles and specialized electric vehicles. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spans over 30,000 square meters and employs more than 200 skilled professionals.

With a focus on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, we've successfully exported our products to over 50 countries across Asia, Africa, Europe, and the Americas. Our commitment to excellence has earned us ISO 9001, CE, and other international certifications.
