About Fugui Springs

Founded in 2005, Fugui Springs has been at the forefront of electric tricycle innovation and manufacturing excellence for over 15 years. With a strong commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, we have grown to become a trusted global provider of electric transportation solutions.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spans over 20,000 square meters and employs more than 200 skilled professionals dedicated to producing premium electric tricycles for diverse applications.
