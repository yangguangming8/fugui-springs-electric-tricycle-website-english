Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our electric tricycles

What is the range of your electric tricycles?

Our electric tricycles have a range of 60-120 km on a single charge, depending on the model and usage conditions. Factors such as load weight, terrain, and driving habits can affect the actual range.

How long does it take to charge the battery?

Standard charging time is 6-8 hours for a full charge. We also offer fast-charging options on select models that can reduce charging time to 3-4 hours.

Do you offer customization options?

Yes, we offer extensive customization options to suit your specific needs. This includes cargo compartment design, seating arrangements, branding, and specialized features for different industries.

What warranty do you provide?

We offer a 2-year warranty on the frame and motor, and a 1-year warranty on the battery. Extended warranty options are also available for purchase.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our electric tricycles worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact our sales team for specific information about shipping to your location.
